FAQs

1.What is Career Counselling? Why is it important?


Career Counselling is defined as a procedure for guiding students (10th standard in our case) for choosing proper stream based on their interests, knowledge and aptitude test. Self-doubt or doubt about career choosing decisions can manipulate you at any point of your life. So, career counselling makes the process choosing stream easier and based on your actual interests. Also, there won’t be any regret about the decisions as you will be fully aware in your mind that you did take the right decision.

2.Is it necessary for me to choose career right now?

Career is one of the most important and highly contributing part of an individual’s life. So, planning about it now is way to go. Many people regret that they lost this golden opportunity of planning their career and having a better life than the present one. Don’t be one of them and plan your career and have a blueprint ready (even a rough one). This will solve your major chunk of issues.

3. What is CareerCouncello’s Assessment Test?

We here at CareerCouncello believe in guiding students by opening them up to all career options and their possibilities. So, our assessment test has been classified in categories namely: Science & Technology, Biology, Commerce, Arts & Literature and Food & Agriculture. Our test’s result will be provided in a graphical way which will help students to judge their choices in a diagrammatical manner. Also, percentage analysis will be provided for each category for clearing your interests category-wise.

4.What is the right time to take Career Counselling?

The fact is that it’s always the right time. The earlier you get this expert guidance, the better you are prepared for your career’s future. These counselling services are most effective when taken before high time. Getting expert advice early in your life gives you more time to explore variety of options in your career and will be more beneficial and effective.

5.Why should I choose CareerCouncello over other career counselling Sites?

Our counselling services include aptitude test, instantaneous graphical test analysis and personal guidance by our counsellors after the test result at the minimal cost available in the market. Also, as mentioned our test includes 5 different and important categories which are rich in questions. These questions are prepared by our counsellors especially considering the viewpoint of the students who’ll be confused about their decision of choosing stream and career. Our test was solely structured on targeting each category of career and also including the wide range of sub-options available in it.

6.What will happen after the test is completed?

Once you are done with the test, the result will be immediately shown to you on the browser. This result will be solely based on the answers chosen by you. After this, if you have selected our advanced test, you will be directed to a website where you will be provided with the link to download the analytical pdf made based on your test result. You will also be able to communicate with our counsellors which will help you with their guidance and they will also solve any doubts that you encounter after completing the test.
